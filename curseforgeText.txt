<p><strong>Who is this person behind that mighty item level 200+ armor? Introduce the real you!&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2>Features</h2>
<h4>Button frame</h4>
<ul>
<li>Woo your favorite player with impressive pick-up lines, or go a little further by being more naughty than nice.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Appreciate your fellow-players with something truly special.</li>
<li>Dance away into the night and sing lyrics from famous Azerothian singers.</li>
<li>Express yourself with catchy phrases.</li>
<li>Don't be swayed away by rude people, have the final word!</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4>Nurse Nancy: Resurrection Messages</h4>
<ul>
<li>A redeemer alternative</li>
<li>Random messages when casting Mass Resurrection, Single Resurrection, Battle ress, soulstones, ankh, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h5><strong>Supported Spells:</strong></h5>
<ul>
<li>Warlock: Soulstone</li>
<li>Paladin: Redemption, Absolution</li>
<li>Priest: Resurrection, Mass Resurrection</li>
<li>Shaman: Ancestral spirit, Reincarnation, Ancestral Vision</li>
<li>Druid: Revive, Rebirth, Revitalize</li>
<li>Monk: Resuscitate, Reawaken</li>
<li>Death Knight: Raise Ally</li>
<li>Hunter: Gift of Chi-ji</li>
<li>Engineering: Goblin Jumper cables, Goblin Jumper cables XL, Gnomish army knife</li>
<li>Other: Darkmoon Card: Twisting Nether</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h5><strong>Side note:</strong></h5>
<p>Nurse Nancy cannot tell in public chat channels as this is hooked to events. This is disabled by Blizzard, so will only be done in instance, raid and party chat.<br />SUUEEGAH!</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4><strong>The Azeroth Answering Machine</strong></h4>
<p>No longer want to be sitting behind your computer to answer all those whispers for you? The answering machine gets rid.. err.. helps<br />you answer your contacts when as they whisper you. Success guaranteed! No goblins or gnomes have been used engineering this machine.</p>
<p>Just do <strong>/afk</strong> or go AFK by default and Express Your Elf it will automatically set a message.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4><strong>Configuration screen</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>Sets the size and position of the Expression Buttons</li>
<li>Activate different features</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>General notice</h3>
<ul>
<li>All expressions are randomized and contextual (based on race, gender and class), to make them more..., you know,... YOU!</li>
<li>Sometimes, when updating this add-on, new features have been added. Which means you may experience a reset of of settings once a while.</li>
</ul>
